Selective Blocking — Not All-or-Nothing

Unlike Do Not Disturb, Permly lets you choose exactly which apps to silence in each situation.

Custom Notification Profiles
Create profiles for any situation — Work, Sleep, Focus, Meetings, Gaming. Each profile silences exactly the apps you choose. Free tier includes 3 profiles; Pro offers unlimited.
Automatic Scheduling Pro
Set start and end times for each profile. Work mode every weekday, sleep mode every night — set it once and forget it.
Selective App Muting
Silence specific apps per profile. Social media during work? Blocked. Calls from loved ones? Always through.
Quick Settings Tile
Toggle your active profile directly from the notification shade — no need to open the app.
Distraction Statistics
Track how many notifications each profile has blocked and which apps interrupt you most. Free: 7-day history. Pro: full history.
No Root Required
Works on any Android 8.0+ device using official Android APIs. Simple setup, no ADB, no sideloading.
No Ads. No Data. Ever.
Permly never reads your notification content — only the app name. All data stays on your device. No analytics, no cloud, no accounts, no tracking. Your attention is not our product.
