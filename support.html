Frequently Asked Questions

How do I grant notification access permission?

To use Permly, you need to grant Notification Access permission:

  1. Open the Permly app
  2. Tap "Grant Permission" on the welcome screen
  3. Find "Permly" in the list and toggle it on
  4. Confirm when prompted

Alternatively, go to: Settings → Apps → Special app access → Notification access → Permly

Why aren't notifications being blocked?

If notifications aren't being blocked, check:

  • Permission granted: Ensure Notification Access is enabled in Android Settings
  • Profile is active: Make sure the profile is toggled ON (green)
  • App is added to profile: Verify the app you want to mute is added to the active profile
  • Service is running: Try toggling the profile off and on again
  • Device restart: Some devices require a restart after granting permission

If issues persist, please contact support with your device model and Android version.

Can I schedule profiles to activate automatically?

Profile scheduling is not available yet but may be added in a future update. Currently, you need to manually toggle profiles on/off. You can use the Quick Settings tile for faster access.

How do I cancel my Pro subscription?

To cancel your Permly Pro subscription:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  3. Find "Permly Pro" and tap it
  4. Tap "Cancel subscription" and follow the prompts

You'll retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. No refunds for partial periods.

For refund inquiries, see our Refund Policy.

Does Permly read my notifications?

No. Permly only reads the package name (app identifier) of incoming notifications to determine whether to block them. We never read notification content, messages, or any personal data.

All data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to servers or shared with third parties.

Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Which Android versions are supported?

Permly requires Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later. This is because the Notification Listener API functionality we rely on was introduced in API level 26.

We test on Android versions 8.0 through 15 (latest).

Why does my battery drain faster?

Permly is designed to be lightweight and should have minimal battery impact. The notification service runs efficiently in the background.

If you notice unusual battery drain:

  • Check if battery optimization is enabled for Permly (Settings → Apps → Permly → Battery)
  • Ensure you're running the latest version
  • Try restarting your device
  • Contact support if the issue persists

Can I use Permly on multiple devices?

Yes! If you have Permly Pro, your subscription is tied to your Google Play account and works on all your Android devices.

However, profiles are stored locally and don't sync between devices. Profile sync is a planned future feature.

How do I use the Quick Settings tile?

The Quick Settings tile lets you toggle profiles without opening the app:

  1. Swipe down from the top of your screen to open Quick Settings
  2. Tap the edit/pencil icon
  3. Find "Permly" and drag it to your active tiles
  4. Tap "Done"

Now you can tap the Permly tile to quickly enable/disable your last active profile.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Fixes

Notifications still appearing

  • Verify Notification Access permission is granted
  • Ensure the profile is active (toggle shows green)
  • Check that the app is added to the active profile
  • Try disabling and re-enabling Notification Access
  • Restart your device

App crashes or freezes

  • Update to the latest version from Google Play
  • Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → Permly → Storage → Clear Cache)
  • If issues persist, try clearing data (this will reset your profiles)
  • Report the issue to us with your device model

Can't create more profiles

  • Free tier is limited to 3 profiles
  • Upgrade to Permly Pro for unlimited profiles
  • Or delete an existing profile to make room

Statistics not updating

  • Statistics are optional - ensure they're enabled in Settings
  • Statistics only track blocks while a profile is active
  • Try toggling statistics off and on in Settings

Data Deletion Instructions

All Permly data is stored locally on your device. To delete your data:

  1. Delete specific profiles: Open Permly → tap a profile → Delete button
  2. Clear all app data: Settings → Apps → Permly → Storage → Clear Data
  3. Complete removal: Uninstall the app (Settings → Apps → Permly → Uninstall)

Since we don't collect or store data on our servers, there's nothing to request deletion of remotely. All data is deleted when you uninstall the app.
